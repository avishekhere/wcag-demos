About This Demo

In real websites, page titles are critical for accessibility and usability. Screen readers read the page title immediately, helping visually impaired users understand the purpose of the page.

Vague titles like "Health" or "Untitled Page" are confusing, while descriptive titles like "National Health Policies | Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India" are meaningful.
